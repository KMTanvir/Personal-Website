Statistical Talks

Invited sessions and workshops

A selection of guest talks and short workshops I have delivered at universities and research institutes across Bangladesh, covering applied statistics, study design, and modern data science tooling.

Sessions delivered

Recent talks

Each session is built around a practical, hands on demonstration suited to the host audience.

01

Modern Data Visualization Techniques

Shahjalal University of Science and Technology
Sylhet, Bangladesh

Principles of effective visual communication, moving from default charts to publication ready graphics with ggplot2 and modern tidy workflows.

Data visualisation ggplot2 Communication
02

Advanced Statistical Tools for Data Analysis

Asian University for Women
Chattogram, Bangladesh

Selecting appropriate methods for real datasets, with worked examples in regression diagnostics, categorical analysis, and reporting uncertainty.

Applied statistics Regression Reproducibility
03

Advanced Sampling Techniques

Jahangirnagar University
Savar, Bangladesh

Stratified, cluster, and multi stage sampling in practice. How survey design choices propagate into variance estimates and policy relevant conclusions.

Survey sampling Design effects Weighting
04

Data Analysis using R and Stata

International Centre for Diarrhoeal Disease Research, Bangladesh (icddr,b)
Dhaka, Bangladesh

A side by side, hands on session for health researchers: cleaning routine data, running common models, and producing reproducible outputs in both R and Stata.

R Stata Applied workflow
05

Developments in Generalized Linear Models

Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University
Dhaka, Bangladesh

Recent developments and practical choices in GLMs for clinical and epidemiological data, including link function selection, overdispersion, and model checking.

GLM Clinical data Model checking
